How-to Change Engine Oil on a Type 1 VW Beetle

DISCLAIMER: THIS IS A STRICTLY AN OILD CHANGE WITHOUT CHANGING THE PICKUP SCREEN. REMEMBER IT'S ALWAYS WISE TO ADJUST YOUR VALVES AT EVERY 3000 MILE INTERVAL ALONG WITH YOUR OIL. THIS SITE DOESN'T COVER THAT PROCEDURE.

STEP 1: Gather Tools and Supplies


Tools List:



- 19mm Combination Wrench - Floor Jack - Jack Stands - Wheel Chocks - Drain Pan - Funnel

Supply List:



- 3 Quarts of Oil - Drain Plug Washer (RECOMMENDED but OPTIONAL)

STEP 2: Raise Car & Secure



- Find Level Surface / Chock Wheels / Jack Up Car / Place Jackstands / Check Stability

STEP 3: Draining Procedures



- Place Drain Pan / Remove Drain Plug / Wait / Replace Washer and Plug / Lower Car

STEP 4: Refilling Procedures



- Remove Fill Cap / Fill With Oil / Check Dipstick

STEP 5: Finishing Procedures



- Check for Leaks, Start Car, Check Oil Pressure Light, Drive
